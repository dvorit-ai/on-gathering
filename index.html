Gathering is is a website that unfolds only through collective presence. Inspired by Mindy Seu’s essay, which explores co-authorship and the idea that relationships between things are a form of authorship, this site reflects that belief: you can only access the full text when enough people are here to reveal it. Alone, you’ll only glimpse fragments.

Alongside the essay, sourced vintage images of plant specimens remind us that the gathering—of words, ideas, and food—is an act of survival, nourishment, and connection.
